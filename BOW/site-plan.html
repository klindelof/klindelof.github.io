Overview

Purpose

Have you ever been invited to a formal event but don't know how to tie a bow tie? Well I have the solution for you! I have created a bow tie that is already folded perfectly with a magnetic piece sewed in the back of the tie so no one will ever have to see it. All you have to do is stick where you want it!

Audience

From ages 2 to 92 these bowties are for everyone! We created this product for everyone to get ready for events in an easier and quicker fashion. This product is designed to grow with you.

FAQs

How do you put it on? Stick It! bowties have a strong magnet on the back. All you have to do is place one magnet where you want it to be, and then put the bowtie on the other side of the clothes. How do I take it off? The magnets won't budge when you pull on it (just in case you are dancing all night) but will easily come off if you slide it one way or the other. Do you have matching options? Yes! We have enough bow ties for everyone in your family to get one! Or if you don't like to match and you out grew your favorite bow tie, we have a bigger option for you! Do you have different styles of bow ties? Of course! We have different styles and patterns, if you go to our styles page you will see a lot of them.
